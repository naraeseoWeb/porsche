  • The Porsche Classic 911 F tool roll.

    With the tailored tool set for your 911 F, you’re sure to always have the right double open-ended wrench on hand. Or the right screwdriver. Just what you need to perform maintenance work on your Porsche yourself while following the instructions in the original driver’s manual. In addition, the roll is made of genuine leather and combines a high-quality, pepita-look design with a high degree of functionality, with each tool in its own fixed slot. The tools have been produced and optimized on the basis of the original genuine parts and are now marked with Porsche lettering. The roll has a fold-out protective flap made of genuine leather to protect your knees from dirt and to enable you to work in a comfortable and relaxed way. The new 911 F tool roll has been tested by our Classic Experts and meets the highest standards for functionality and quality. That’s why the tool roll bears the Porsche Crest from the earlier 911 horn button.

  • This tool roll contains the following tools:

    - Double open-ended spanners 8x9 & 10x11 & 12x13
    - Double open-ended spanners 14x15 & 17x19
    - Wheel nut wrench
    - Holding tool for V-belt pulley
    - Combination pliers
    - Ring spanner
    - Phillips screwdriver
    - Screwdriver
    - Spark plug wrench
    - Spark plug ratchet wrench
    - Hexagon socket wrench
    - Brush
    - Feeler gauge
    - V-belt
    - Cleaning cloth

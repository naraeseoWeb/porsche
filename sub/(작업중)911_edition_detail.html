Design of the model

50 Years of Porsche Design.

In 1972, Prof. F. A. Porsche - the Designer of the iconic 911 - was hit by a stroke of genius – the matt black Chronograph I, which continues to inspire Porsche Design's timepieces to this day. Fifty years later, the Chronograph 1 – 911 Edition 50 Years Porsche Design is reminiscent of the design of this legendary timepiece that started a new era.

More features

More highlights

  • 'PORSCHE' lettering. The characteristic 'PORSCHE' lettering on the rear is timelessly elegant with a matt black finish, just like in the model designation.
  • 50 Years of Porsche Design plaque on the rear. A real eye-catcher: the plaques on the ventilation grilles with high-gloss black fins combined with the 50 Years of Porsche Design logo with the signature of F. A. Porsche .
  • Door-sill guards. Puristic and elegant: the brushed door-sill guards with an integrated 50 Years of Porsche Design Logo with black anodised aluminium finish.
  • Embossed headrests. High-quality: the ergonomic headrests are embossed with the 50 Years of Porsche Design logo.
  • Sport Chrono Package Plus. Stylish and timeless: the Sport Chrono Package Plus with a high-quality watch from Porsche Design . The seconds hand is red, the dial references the look of the Chronograph 1 – 911 Edition 50 Years of Porsche Design.
  • There can only be one. Limited edition plaque with '911' lettering and individual limited edition numbering on the right of the dashboard.

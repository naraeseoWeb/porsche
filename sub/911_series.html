The anniversary model has a limited run of 750 units and features a timeless sports car design with the standout elements of an iconic timepiece. Car Shapes.

  • Car body shapes.

    Coupé.

    ─ The Coupé version of the 911 embodies Porsche DNA in its purest form: the long, flat bonnet, the steeply inclined windscreen. And the roof line that slopes gently down towards the rear, already characterized the original 911.

  • Car body shapes.

    Cabriolet.

    ─ The open-top variant of the 911 has always been characterised by a heightened feeling of freedom and plenty of fresh air. When closed, the Cabriolet has the elegant silhouette of the 911 Coupé models – thanks to its fully automatic fabric hood.

  • Car body shapes.

    Targa.

    ─ The most stylish 911 model has the addition ‘targa’. With its characteristic Targa bar and innovative roof concept, the 911 Targa is a timeless classic. And at the same time it is an open-top variant of the 911 with the comfort of an enclosed car.

Sound System

BOSE® Surround Sound System.

12 loudspeakers and amplifier channels with 100-watt subwoofers integral to the vehicle bodyshell characterize the BOSE® Surround Sound System. The total output: 570 watts. The BOSE® SoundTrue Enhancement Technology ensures high audio quality and dynamics for compressed file formats.
